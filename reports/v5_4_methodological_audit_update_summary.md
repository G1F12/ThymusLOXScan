# v5.4 methodological audit update summary

## Stronger result

The matched-gene falsification result remains useful: Loxl2-like collapse was uncommon among similarly expressed mTEC1 genes. This supports keeping Loxl2 prioritized as a transcript-level candidate pattern within the GSE240016 mTEC1 analysis.

## Still unresolved

- The mTEC1 design remains n=2 vs n=2 biological samples.
- Exact permutation p-value resolution is limited to six possible labelings.
- Aged mTEC1 samples have lower depth and fewer detected genes.
- Residual sample/batch confounding may remain because local metadata lack run, library date, mouse ID, and batch fields.
- Baran-Gale remains directional/provisional because true per-animal aggregation was not verified.

## Baran-Gale classification

**provisional directional context: sample identity uncertain**

The Baran-Gale Loxl2 pattern is mostly decreasing across broad TEC, mTEC-like, mTEClo, mTEChi, and cTEC sample-proxy summaries, but it should not be used as exact or subtype-specific confirmation.

## Recommendation

A manuscript update is **not recommended yet** if the goal is to add stronger Baran-Gale support. The Baran-Gale result may be included only as supplement/context with explicit provisional wording, or held back until R-based export and per-animal metadata verification are complete.

No release is recommended from this audit alone.
